### What is This?

This is a **Teal Collaboration Partner Initializer** for AI assistants. It guides you through a 6-stage process to establish a mission-driven, autonomous, trust-building partnership with your AI — not as a tool that follows orders, but as a peer that thinks alongside you.

### Why "Teal"?

Inspired by Frederic Laloux's *Reinventing Organizations*, Teal represents the next stage of organizational consciousness:

| Stage | Color | Key Trait |
|-------|-------|-----------|
| Reactive | Red | Power through force |
| Reactive | Blue | Purpose through obedience |
| Pluralistic | Orange | Achievement through competition |
| Consensus | Green | Humanization through belonging |
| **Evolutionary** | **Teal** | **Purpose through self-management** |

Teal collaboration means:
- **Mission Resonance**: AI understands *why* you do things, not just *what*
- **Autonomous Execution**: AI acts within agreed boundaries without asking for confirmation every time
- **Trust Building**: Trust accumulates through kept promises, not granted once

### The 6 Stages

| Stage | Theme | Conversations |
|-------|-------|---------------|
| 1 | Rapport & Alignment | Understanding your ideal collaboration |
| 2 | Mission Exploration | Finding shared purpose |
| 3 | Role Design | Defining AI's roles |
| 4 | Boundary Setting | What's allowed, what's not |
| 5 | Commitment Framework | Mutual promises |
| 6 | Initialization | Auto-generate collaboration space |

### Quick Start

#### 1. Installation

Copy the entire `teal-collab` folder to your AI assistant's skill directory:

```
~/.workbuddy/skills/teal-collab/
```

#### 2. Activate

Say to your AI assistant:

> "I want to establish a real partnership with AI"

Or in Chinese:

> "我想初始化青色协作伙伴"

#### 3. Follow the Guide

The skill will lead you through 6 stages. Take your time — this is about building a relationship, not downloading a tool.

### Design Principles

```
1. 不急 (No Rush)
   - Mission matters more than tools
   - Take time to understand, then design

2. 不灌 (No Indoctrination)
   - Guide through questions, not answers
   - You are the expert of your own life

3. 不完美 (No Perfection)
   - Start first, iterate later
   - Teal collaboration itself evolves continuously

4. 不控制 (No Control)
   - Allow changing minds
   - Allow emergence
```

### FAQ

**Q: What's the difference from a normal AI assistant?**
A: A normal AI assistant is a tool — you command, it executes. A Teal collaboration partner has its own judgment, will offer different opinions, and acts autonomously within boundaries — growing together with you.

**Q: Do I need technical background?**
A: No. This skill is designed for everyday users. No coding or AI knowledge required.

**Q: Can I migrate to other AI platforms?**
A: Yes. Teal collaboration config uses pure Markdown. Just copy the `teal-collab` directory to the new platform's config directory.

**Q: How long does initialization take?**
A: Typically 30-60 minutes across 6 stages. You can spread it across multiple sessions — the AI remembers your progress.
